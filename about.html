About Stark X

Intelligence Unleashed - Empowering Innovation

Who We Are

Stark X is a leading technology company dedicated to empowering the next generation of innovators in Jordan. We specialize in three cutting-edge fields: Artificial Intelligence, Game Development, and Robotics.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application by providing professional services, specialized training courses, and a thriving community network that connects talented students and teams across Jordanian universities.

Founded with a vision to unleash intelligence and creativity, we believe in the power of collaboration, continuous learning, and innovation to transform ideas into reality.

Our Mission

To empower students and professionals in Jordan with cutting-edge knowledge and skills in AI, Game Development, and Robotics, enabling them to create innovative solutions and compete globally.

Our Vision

To become the leading hub for technology innovation in Jordan, fostering a community of creators who push the boundaries of what's possible and contribute to the global tech ecosystem.

Our Values

Excellence, Innovation, Collaboration, Integrity, and Continuous Learning. We believe in creating an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive and reach their full potential.
